    Game rules

    Monster combat

    • Matching monsters remove each other.
    • If all three monster types meet, all of them are removed.

    Match rules

    • Each player may deploy, move, or skip once per round.
    • Move straight any distance or diagonally up to two cells.
    • Friendly monsters can be jumped. Enemies stop movement on the nearest free cell.
    • Opposing movements cannot pass through each other.
    • Lose 10 monsters and you are eliminated.
    • Skip five consecutive turns and you are eliminated.
